Join Our Community

We welcome all UW students, regardless of discipline or experience level, to join the Data Science Society at UW. Members gain access to exclusive events, workshops, and a network of AI enthusiasts.

How to Join

Becoming a member is easy! Attend one of our events or meetings, and sign up using the provided registration form. You can also join via our online platform.

  • Attend our weekly meetings
  • Fill out the registration form
  • Join our online community

Member Benefits

As a member, you'll enjoy exclusive access to our resources and community.

  • Priority access to AI workshops and seminars
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Participation in AI projects and research
  • Access to exclusive member-only events
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Career development resources

Our Community

Join a diverse community of students passionate about data science and AI.

  • Connect with like-minded peers
  • Collaborate on exciting projects
  • Learn from experienced members
  • Build lasting professional relationships

Ready to Join?

Don't miss out on the opportunity to be part of our vibrant data science community. Join us today and start your journey in AI and data science!
