Membership

We welcome all UW students, regardless of discipline or experience level, to join the Data Science Society at UW. Members gain access to exclusive events, workshops, and a network of AI enthusiasts.

How to Join

To become a member, attend one of our events or meetings, and sign up using the provided registration form. You can also join via our online platform.

Member Benefits

  • Priority access to AI workshops and seminars
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Participation in AI projects and research
